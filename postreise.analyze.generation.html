Calculate individual generator costs at given powers. If decommit is True, costs will be zero below the decommit threshold (1 MW).

Parameters

  • gencost (pandas.DataFrame) – cost curve polynomials.

  • decommit (boolean) – Whether to decommit generator at very low power.

Returns

postreise.analyze.generation.emissions.generate_emissions_stats(scenario, pollutant='carbon', method='simple')[source]¶

Generate emissions statistics from the input generation data. Method descriptions: ‘simple’ uses a fixed ratio of CO2 to MWh, ‘always-on’ uses generator heat-rate curves including non-zero intercepts, ‘decommit’ uses generator heat-rate curves but de-commits generators if they are off (detected by pg < 1 MW).

Parameters

  • scenario (powersimdata.scenario.scenario.Scenario) – scenario instance.

  • pollutant (str) – pollutant to analyze.

  • method (str) – selected method to handle no-load fuel consumption.

Returns

(pandas.DataFrame) – emissions data frame.
